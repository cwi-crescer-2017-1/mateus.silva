Atlas Shrugged

If you don't know, the thing to do is not get scared, but to learn.
People think that a liar gains a victory over his victim. What I’ve learned is that a lie is an act of making that person one’s master, self-abdication, because one surrenders one’s reality to the person to whom one lies, condemning oneself from then on to faking the sort of reality that person’s view requires to be faked… The man who lies to the world, is the world’s slave from then on… There are no white lies, there is only the blackest of destruction, and a white lie is the blackest of all.
